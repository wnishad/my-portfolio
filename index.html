Project — Churn Prediction (example)

Data: Customer activity logs + subscription records.

Goal: Predict customers likely to churn within 30 days to target retention campaigns.

Challenges: Data sparsity, class imbalance, and concept drift across quarters.

Solution: Feature engineering (recency/frequency), SMOTE for balancing, ensemble model (XGBoost) with time-based validation. Produced model dashboard and exportable scoring pipeline.

Project — Sales Forecasting (example)

Data: Historical sales, promotions, and calendar data.

Goal: Improve short-term demand forecasts to optimize inventory.

Challenges: Seasonality, promotion-driven spikes, and missing values.

Solution: Time-series cross validation, FB Prophet and LSTM baselines, feature-based regression with holidays and promo indicators, and dashboarding results for business users.
